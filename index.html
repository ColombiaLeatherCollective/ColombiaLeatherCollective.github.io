Colombia Leather Collective

Exquisitely handcrafted leather goods from Colombia,
designed to last a lifetime.

Our Mission

Colombia Leather Collective is a curator of the highest quality leather goods in the world. We specifically partner with Colombian leatherworkers to help them gain access to the new markets where there is demand for intricately designed, handcrafted leather goods.

Goods that Last a Lifetime

Products are often designed to fail after a specific amount of time to create recurring revenue for businesses. We believe this is wrong and unsustainable. Colombia Leather Collective strives to be a counterpoint to this business practice, procuring leather goods which are designed to last a lifetime, helping contribute to a more sustainable buying economy.

A Documented Supply Chain

There is not enough effort on the side of businesses to inform consumers of the full supply chain of their products. This has led to an economy where we no longer understand where our possesions come from. We aim to inform our customers of the full supply chain of our leather products, so that they individually know the origin story of their leather goods.

A Personal Buying Process

We believe that creating a personal connection between suppliers, vendors, and consumers leads to a more informed economy, and a more authentic, less manufactured buying process. Colombia Leather Collective is in the people business, we just happen to sell leather goods.

Roadmap to a Sustainable Future

Our goal is to sustainably scale into a business that relies on individual producers in both Colombia and the United States, rather than building our business model around mass-producers. We believe that the path towards sustainability is by being deliberate about supporting local economies, businesses, and entrepreneurs.
